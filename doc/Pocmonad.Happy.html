    pocmonad v0.1.0 Pocmonad.Happy

    This is the Happy module, Ok, so I was just trying to find a nice way (beautiful syntax, yet flexible enough) to handle errors in elixir. Handling :ok/:error like tuples without lots of if/cases.

    HEX

    {:happy, "~> 1.3"}

    Pros :

    • Clean to read
    • Its kind of with but less oppinied

    Cons :

    • Implementation given for other people
    • Not follow the pipeline way
    • sounds like framework and not lib
